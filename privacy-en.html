PRIVACY POLICY — PRÉHOS RXSCAN

Version: 1.0

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Préhos RxScan ("RxScan", "the Application") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of information processed through the use of the Application.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

2. Information Collected

Technical Information

The Application may collect certain technical information necessary for its operation, including:

  • application version;
  • accepted consent version;
  • selected language;
  • date and time of use;
  • application-generated technical identifiers;
  • error and diagnostic information.

Optional Information Submitted Through the Improvement Program

When a user voluntarily participates in the RxScan Improvement Program, certain information may be transmitted to improve the service, including:

  • analyzed photographs;
  • generated PDF documents;
  • OCR results;
  • detected medications;
  • analyses produced by the Application;
  • technical logs;
  • Scan IDs;
  • processing date and time.

Participation in this program is entirely optional.

3. Use of Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • improve medication recognition;
  • improve OCR performance;
  • correct software defects;
  • improve system stability;
  • develop new features;
  • analyze processing errors;
  • maintain service security and integrity.

Information is not used for advertising purposes.

4. Consent

Certain features rely on user consent.

Participation in the Improvement Program is entirely optional and may be declined without affecting normal use of the Application.

Accepted consent records may be stored locally to maintain an audit trail of user permissions.

5. Data Retention

Information submitted through the Improvement Program may be retained as long as reasonably necessary for:

  • issue investigation;
  • product improvement;
  • validation of fixes;
  • legitimate development purposes.

Information may be deleted when no longer required.

6. Hosting and Security

Information may be stored using cloud services utilized by Préhos RxScan to operate the Application.

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect information against:

  • unauthorized access;
  • loss;
  • disclosure;
  • unauthorized modification.

However, no information system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Information Sharing

Préhos RxScan does not sell user information.

Information may be shared only:

  • with technical service providers required to operate the service;
  • when required by law;
  • when necessary to protect the legal rights of Préhos RxScan.

8. Withdrawal of Consent

Users may withdraw their consent to participate in the Improvement Program at any time when such functionality is available within the Application.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

9. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of voluntarily submitted information by contacting Préhos RxScan.

Certain information may be retained where required by law or necessary to protect legal rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

Préhos RxScan may update this Privacy Policy at any time.

The version in effect will be the version published within the Application or on the official documentation pages.
